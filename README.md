#OMLETTE

ingredients

2eggs
milk 
salt
greeen onion
green chilly
tomatoe
coriander
cheese

process
mix all ingredients
heat up some oil in pan
let it heat up
turn the flame to low
pour half of the egg in the pan
let it cook for 2-3 mins flip the omlette and cook it for 300 secs
do the same for second omlette
toast some bread and enjoy
